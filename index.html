Dropbox DropQuest2011

January 15, 2011

I just finished the Dropquest 2011 that was created during the Dropbox Hack-week. Full of little odd puzzles it was a joy to do. I didn't finish anywhere near some of the first, but I managed to complete it in under 1.5h and I'm quite satisfied with that. All in all it netted me an extra 1 GB of space in my dropbox which is always welcome; I'm still using the free plan.

Thanks to the Dropbox team for creating this little adventure, and thanks in general for being such an awesome application. Here's to hoping that Dropquest will return next year :)
